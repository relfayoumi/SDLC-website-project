SDLC-stages - Testing and Integration

After all of the previous stages of the software development life cycle have been completed, quality assurance engineers begin looking for bugs. Testing is another critical step in the SDLC life cycle because it allows for the resolution of critical issues before they cause critical losses. Although testing procedures may appear in other stages of the software development life cycle, testing provides a detailed map of breakdowns that must be addressed.

To effectively test a product before launch, first define your product's success criteria. Next, determine your target audience and develop a prototype or minimum viable product (MVP). Conduct usability and functionality tests, and solicit feedback from your target audience.
