SDLC-stages - Deployment and Maintenance

After the product is release-ready, the next action in the order of software development life cycle steps is to display the finished software solution to the client. To guarantee the proper work of the finished digital solution in the future, after all the SDLC life cycle phases are completed, the client can order general product maintenance to fix different issues (which inevitably emerge with any modern software product). This step completes the software development life cycle.

Deployment and maintenance are crucial phases of the software development life cycle (SDLC) that guarantee the quality, reliability, and availability of your software items. However, they can also consume a lot of resources and money, especially in the event that you have complex, distributed, or frequently updated systems.

Why is Deployment & Maintenance important?

a. To make sure not to waste resources

b. To make sure the customer is satisfied

c. To make sure everything works and fits the customers needs

d. To understand what the customer is asking for
